The Problem

Most small businesses do not lose leads because of bad service. They lose them because follow-up is messy.

Busy owners miss calls

Customers call while the owner is driving, working on a job, meeting a client, or handling the next appointment.

DMs get buried

Instagram, WhatsApp, text messages, and email can all become separate inboxes with no clear next step.

Forms wait too long

Website forms are useful, but slow replies make it easy for a customer to contact another business first.

Quotes are not followed up

A quote might get sent, but without a simple reminder flow, the opportunity can quietly go cold.

Reviews are forgotten

Happy customers often leave no review because nobody asks at the right time in a consistent way.

Lead info gets scattered

Phone, text, Instagram, WhatsApp, email, paper notes, and memory are not a reliable lead system.

The System

A simple system that captures, organizes, and follows up.

The goal is not to make your business complicated. It is to make every inquiry easier to see, respond to, organize, and follow up on.

I build around the way your business already works, then add the missing structure: capture, confirmation, intake, dashboard, booking or quote follow-up, review requests, and monthly support.
