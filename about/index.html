About

Built by Hamid.

I build practical software for real workflows: dashboards, automations, web tools, and local business systems.

I am Hamid, a systems and automation builder based in the GTA. My focus is helping small service businesses turn messy customer inquiries into organized, trackable, follow-up-ready workflows.

I like systems that earn their place: clear status, simple controls, readable dashboards, practical follow-up, and pages that make the next step obvious.

Hamid Systems / Samadi Systems is built around a direct idea: local businesses should not lose good customers just because the owner was busy, the DM got buried, or the quote was never followed up.

Principles

How I approach local business systems.

Start with the workflow

Before building anything, map how leads arrive, who responds, and where follow-up breaks down.

Keep it lightweight

Use the simplest setup that can reliably capture, organize, and follow up on customer inquiries.

Make it easy to run

The system should still make sense when the business is busy and the owner needs the next step fast.
